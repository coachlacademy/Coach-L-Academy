Course Curriculum

  • Week 1: Understanding Narcissism
    Introduction to narcissistic personality disorder, the spectrum of narcissism, and distinguishing between healthy confidence and pathological narcissism.
  • Week 2: The Cycle of Narcissistic Abuse
    Learn about idealization, devaluation, and discard phases. Understand why the cycle repeats and how trauma bonding develops.
  • Week 3: Manipulation Tactics Unveiled
    Deep dive into gaslighting, triangulation, projection, love bombing, silent treatment, and other common manipulation strategies.
  • Week 4: Impact on Self-Esteem and Identity
    Understand how narcissistic abuse affects your sense of self, self-worth, and mental health. Recognize cognitive dissonance and learned helplessness.
  • Week 5: Setting Boundaries and Self-Protection
    Practical strategies for setting firm boundaries, grey rock technique, going no-contact or low-contact, and protecting your peace.
  • Week 6: Moving Forward with Awareness
    Creating your action plan, recognizing red flags in future relationships, building a support system, and committing to your healing journey.
