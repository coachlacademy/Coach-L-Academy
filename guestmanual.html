4. Joining the Live Video Chat with Host

This is the most important part of your guest experience.
  1. Scroll to the Live Video Chat with Host window
  2. Click the link that says ‚ÄúJoin in Browser‚Äù
  3. You will be asked to enter your name
    • Type your real name or DJ name
    • No password is required here
  4. Allow access to your microphone (and camera if requested)
  5. You will now enter the Live Host Video

5. Using the Chat Feature

The Live Video Chat includes a built-in chat feature.
  • Open the chat panel inside the video window
  • You can:
    • See messages from the host
    • See messages from listeners (if enabled)
    • Send text messages to the host during the show
  • Use chat if:
    • You have audio issues
    • You need to coordinate with the host quietly
    • You want to respond to comments

6. On-Air Rules & Best Practices

  • Use headphones to avoid echo
  • Mute your microphone when not speaking
  • Follow host instructions at all times
  • Do not share guest login details

7. Ending Your Session

When the live call ends:
  • You may be returned to standby automatically
  • You can safely close the browser tab
  • Do not rejoin unless instructed by the host
